Popular Questions

CAN I AMEND MY ORDER?

Tickets are sold on a non-transferable basis unless the event has significantly changed. If you would like to request an exchange then please provide the full details of the date/time you wish to exchange to via out contact form here and ensure we have tickets available for the date/time you have requested by checking the availability online.
Please note that this will be a request only and we cannot offer a guarantee that your request will be actioned.

DO I HAVE TO PRINT OUT MY TICKETS IF I HAVE PDF TICKETS?

You do not need to physically print out your tickets and these can be scanned on your phone without issue.

FORGOTTEN BOOKING REFERENCE NUMBER?

Your reference number is displayed in the subject of the confirmation email, and again under your name and event name.

If you are unsure of your booking reference number you can enter your email address in the Forgotten Details section at the bottom of the page here.
We will send you the details of any orders linked to that email which will then take you through to the customer service area and you can track your order. Please ensure this email matches the email address you entered on your booking.

I AM DUE A REFUND BUT I HAVE NEW CARD DETAILS.

We must attempt to process the refund on to the original payment card. If the card you used has changed or expired but the account is still active then the funds will be returned directly to your account. If we are unable to refund the amount we will contact you for new details.

CAN I CHANGE THE NAME ON MY ORDER?

If the event is not name specific then no name change is required and you will be perfectly fine to gift your tickets to family/friends.
If the event is name specific then the name cannot be amended and the lead booker will need to attend the event with ID to gain entry to the event. If you are unsure if the event is name specific the please check the more information section of the booking page, your confirmation email and our support centre.

HOW DO I CHANGE MY EMAIL ADDRESS?

Please log in to our customer service area here and select the change address/email address option.

HOW DO I CHANGE MY ADDRESS?

Please log in to our customer service area here and select the change address/email address option.

CAN I ADD EXTRA TICKETS TO MY ORDER?

Please check the booking page for the event as there may be an option to add tickets to your previous order. If this option is not available for the event you have booked for then tickets will need to be purchased as a new transaction.
