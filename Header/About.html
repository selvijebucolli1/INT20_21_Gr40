About TMP

Ticket Marketplace is where fans go for all their ticket options in one marketplace. Powered by TMP, we provide a safe and secure online marketplace that
gives fans easy access to a wide range of ticket products at market driven prices. For more information on what tickets we have on our website please read below.

ULTIMATE – The best seats in the house

Ticket Marketplace is where fans go for the best seats in the house. Want to get up and close to the artist or have the ultimate experience? We exclusively offer the best seats
allocated by the Promoter at market driven prices, giving all fans fair and safe access to the most in-demand tickets right up to the event.



DEALS – Hot offers on last minute tickets

Ticket Marketplace is where fans go for hot offers on last minute tickets. Get exclusive access to hot offers on tickets to events that are happening very soon!
Score great deals on your tickets at the last minute and enjoy more live music and entertainment.



FAN TO FAN – Buy and sell real tickets

Ticket Marketplace is where fans go to buy and sell real tickets. Our innovative platform gives fans easy access to exchange tickets safely and securely. All tickets on Ticket Marketplace
are capped at a maximum price of 10% above face value. Every ticket exchanged is reissued into the name of the new owner and guarantees fans entry to their event. Ticket Marketplace
gives fans the chance to sell their tickets to other fans for events they can no longer attend.



Extra Information

With over 35 years' experience ticketing the biggest live events and venues in Australia, TMP is the leading ticketing partner to the sports and live entertainment industry and operates Australia's
most advanced multi-channel ticket sales and distribution network. A truly digital company, TMP.com.au is Australia's number one Entertainment Events website, with a market leading mobile
platform that gives customers the ability to browse, buy and deliver tickets on their phone, through both the mobile site and iPhone app. Ticket Marketplace also offers a dedicated team of call
centre representatives, and operates 114 agency outlets throughout Australia. TMP sells over 23 million tickets to more than 20,000 events each year, including concerts, sports, theatre, musicals,
festivals, exhibitions, experience and family events. TMP provides ticketing services to Australia's premier venues including ANZ Stadium, Qudos Bank Arena, Sydney Cricket Ground, Allianz
Stadium, Melbourne Cricket Ground, Rod Laver Arena, Hisense Arena, AAMI Park, Her Majesty's Theatre, Blundstone Arena, Suncorp Stadium, Brisbane Entertainment Centre, Gold Coast
Convention and Exhibition Centre, Newcastle Entertainment Centre, Canberra Stadium, National Gallery of Australia Canberra, Adelaide Entertainment Centre, Adelaide Oval, Perth Theatre
Trust, Regal Theatre and Perth Arena. Ticket Marketplace also sells tickets to some of Australia's most prominent events including the Australian Open Tennis Tournament. TMP Australia has
offices in Sydney, Melbourne, Adelaide, Brisbane, Canberra, the Gold Coast and Perth.
