About TMP

With over 35 years' experience ticketing the biggest live events and venues in Australia, TMP is the leading ticketing partner to the sports and live entertainment industry and operates Australia's most advanced multi-channel ticket sales and distribution network. A truly digital company, TMP.com.au is Australia's number one Entertainment Events website, with a market leading mobile platform that gives customers the ability to browse, buy and deliver tickets on their phone, through both the mobile site and iPhone app. TicketMarketplace also offers a dedicated team of call centre representatives, and operates 114 agency outlets throughout Australia. TMP sells over 23 million tickets to more than 20,000 events each year, including concerts, sports, theatre, musicals, festivals, exhibitions, experience and family events. TMP provides ticketing services to Australia's premier venues including ANZ Stadium, Qudos Bank Arena, Sydney Cricket Ground, Allianz Stadium, Melbourne Cricket Ground, Rod Laver Arena, Hisense Arena, AAMI Park, Her Majesty's Theatre, Blundstone Arena, Suncorp Stadium, Brisbane Entertainment Centre, Gold Coast Convention and Exhibition Centre, Newcastle Entertainment Centre, Canberra Stadium, National Gallery of Australia Canberra, Adelaide Entertainment Centre, Adelaide Oval, Perth Theatre Trust, Regal Theatre and Perth Arena. Ticketek also sells tickets to some of Australia's most prominent events including the Australian Open Tennis Tournament. TMP Australia has offices in Sydney, Melbourne, Adelaide, Brisbane, Canberra, the Gold Coast and Perth.
